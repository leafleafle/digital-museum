PAST (2000-2010)


Reality shows became a popular point of topic and discussion among Filipinos. Pinoy Big Brother on the ABS-CBN channel was deliberately framed as a "social experiment" show. People felt involved in the trajectory of shows like PBB since they were encouraged to vote through text messaging and often discussed things like "Who should be evicted?" with each other in-person and on forums like PinoyExchange.

PRESENT (2011-2025)


Until now, Pinoy Big Brother is a popular and ongoing show, with the latest season (ran from March to July 2025) having done exceptionally well, especially since ABS-CBN collaborated with GMA network. PinoyExchange has now shut down, but discussions of PBB favorites have now mostly moved to Twitter/X, with contest names names like Mika Salamanca and Bianca de Vera trending frequently on the app during its airing.
